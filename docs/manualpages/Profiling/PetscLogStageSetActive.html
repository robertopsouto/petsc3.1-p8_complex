PetscLogStageSetActive

 Determines stage activity for PetscLogEventBegin() and PetscLogEventEnd().

Synopsis

#include "petscsys.h"   
PetscErrorCode PETSC_DLLEXPORT PetscLogStageSetActive(PetscLogStage stage, PetscTruth isActive)
Not Collective

Input Parameters

stage - The stage
isActive - The activity flag, PETSC_TRUE for logging, else PETSC_FALSE (defaults to PETSC_TRUE)

See Also

PetscLogStagePush(), PetscLogStagePop(), PetscLogEventBegin(), PetscLogEventEnd(), PreLoadBegin(), PreLoadEnd(), PreLoadStage()
