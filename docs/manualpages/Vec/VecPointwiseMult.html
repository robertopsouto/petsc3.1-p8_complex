VecPointwiseMult

 Computes the componentwise multiplication w = x*y.

Synopsis

#include "petscvec.h" 
PetscErrorCode  VecPointwiseMult(Vec w, Vec x,Vec y)
Collective on Vec

Input Parameters

x, y -the vectors

Output Parameter

w -the result

Notes: any subset of the x, y, and w may be the same vector.

C++ variants

  VecPointwiseMult(Vec x,Vec y)->VecPointwiseMult(x,x,y)
