DMGetInterpolation

 Gets interpolation matrix between two DA or DMComposite objects

Synopsis

#include "petscda.h"     
PetscErrorCode PETSCDM_DLLEXPORT DMGetInterpolation(DM dm1,DM dm2,Mat *mat,Vec *vec)
Collective on DM

Input Parameter

dm1 - the DM object
dm2 - the second, finer DM object

Output Parameter

mat - the interpolation
vec - the scaling (optional)

See Also

DMDestroy(), DMView(), DMCreateGlobalVector(), DMGetColoring(), DMGetMatrix()
