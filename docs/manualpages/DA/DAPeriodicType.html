DAPeriodicType

 Is the domain periodic in one or more directions

Synopsis

typedef enum { DA_NONPERIODIC,DA_XPERIODIC,DA_YPERIODIC,DA_XYPERIODIC,
               DA_XYZPERIODIC,DA_XZPERIODIC,DA_YZPERIODIC,DA_ZPERIODIC,DA_XYZGHOSTED} DAPeriodicType;

DA_XYZGHOSTED means that ghost points are put around all the physical boundaries in the local representation of the Vec (i.e. DACreate/GetLocalVector().
