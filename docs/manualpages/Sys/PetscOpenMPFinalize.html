PetscOpenMPFinalize

 Finalizes the PETSc and MPI to work with OpenMP. Called by PetscFinalize() cannot be called by user.

Synopsis

#include "petscsys.h"   
PetscErrorCode PETSC_DLLEXPORT PetscOpenMPFinalize(void)
Collective on the entire system
