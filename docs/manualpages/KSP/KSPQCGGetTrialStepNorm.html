KSPQCGGetTrialStepNorm

 Gets the norm of a trial step vector. The WCG step may be constrained, so this is not necessarily the length of the ultimate step taken in QCG.

Synopsis

#include "petscksp.h" 
PetscErrorCode PETSCKSP_DLLEXPORT KSPQCGGetTrialStepNorm(KSP ksp,PetscReal *tsnorm)
Collective on KSP

Input Parameter

ksp -the iterative context

Output Parameter

tsnorm -the norm

Level:advanced
