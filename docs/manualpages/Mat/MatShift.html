MatShift

 Computes Y = Y + a I, where a is a PetscScalar and I is the identity matrix.

Synopsis

#include "petscmat.h"  
PetscErrorCode PETSCMAT_DLLEXPORT MatShift(Mat Y,PetscScalar a)
Collective on Mat

Input Parameters

Y - the matrices
a - the PetscScalar

Keywords

matrix, add, shift
